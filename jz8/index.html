Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for work?

Click on any job posting you're interested in, then use the WhatsApp button to contact the employer directly.

Is this job confirmed?

Yes, we verify all employers and job listings before they are published to our platform.

What if I find this job too difficult?

All our job opportunities have detailed standard operating procedures, no minimum entry requirements, anyone can quickly master within an hour. If you still feel challenged, tell us your strengths and we'll find suitable work for you from our many part-time opportunities.

How do I decide which part-time job is best for me?

When choosing part-time work, you can decide based on your skills, schedule and expected income. Each job listing details required skills, expected income and user reviews. If you're a beginner, we recommend starting with free or low-cost work, then considering higher-income projects after familiarizing yourself with the process.

How can I avoid being scammed?

Be wary of unrealistic promises like "high pay without work" jobs. Verify the credibility of job providers by checking reviews and conducting background research. Never share sensitive personal information like passwords or ID numbers. Report any suspicious requests to customer service immediately.
